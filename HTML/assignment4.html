In human-robot collaboration, the robot assists
the human operator. This means: The machine
does not replace the human, but complements
his capabilities and relieves him of arduous tasks.
These can include overhead work, for example,
or the lifting of heavy loads. Autonomous,
collaborative robots are also used to supply
production workstations.
